Our Story

From a Backyard Dream to Luton's Finest

Nearly 25 years ago, Mr. Iftikhar Ahmad began a journey that would soon become a culinary legacy known as Hassan Catering.

It all started in his own backyard, with a vision, a passion for genuine flavours, and the belief that great food has the power to bring people together. With nothing more than a few family recipes and a dedication to quality, he began fulfilling orders one by one, creating dishes that echoed the traditional flavours of home.

Back then, there were no grand kitchens or sophisticated equipment — just Mr. Iftikhar's skillful hands and his unwavering commitment to perfection. Every order he prepared was crafted with love and care, ensuring that each dish was nothing less than exceptional. Word spread quickly about the delicious food coming out of his backyard, and soon, what started as a small endeavour grew beyond his wildest dreams.

Today, Hassan Catering is a household brand synonymous with delicious and genuine taste. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, families and businesses alike trust Hassan Catering to deliver the flavours they love.
